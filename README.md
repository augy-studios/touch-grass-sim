# Touch Grass Simulator

> Sometimes you just need to touch some grass.

Go outside. Explore. Discover hidden things. Touch some grass!

---

## Features

- **Grass simulation** — blades react to your cursor/touch with a diamond-shaped interaction area
- **Time of day** — sky, grass colour, and ambient sounds shift automatically based on your real local time (dawn → morning → day → dusk → evening → night)
- **Live weather** — fetches real wind speed via Open-Meteo using your location; grass sways and leans with the actual wind, and rain falls if it's raining outside
- **Discoveries** — move through the grass to uncover hidden objects, rated common / uncommon / rare, each generated by Claude AI and tailored to your location and time of day
- **Scoring** — common finds worth 1 pt, uncommon 2 pts, rare 3 pts
- **Global leaderboard** — submit your name and score to a live Supabase leaderboard at the end of each session
- **Ambient audio** — wind gusts, bird chirps, and rain sounds that respond to real weather conditions
